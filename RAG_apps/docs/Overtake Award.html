Formula 1 Award
Overtake Award
Reward(s)Trophy
First awarded2021
Currently held byCharles Leclerc
WebsiteOfficial website

The Overtake Award is an award introduced in 2021 Formula One season to reward the individual driver who makes the most overtakes throughout the season. It is currently named after Crypto.com, one of the main sponsors of Formula One.[1]

For the 2022 season, the award was modified: the amount of overtakes did not count anymore, instead the fans voted for the best overtake of each Grand Prix via social media. At the end of the season there was a social media poll to determine the overall winner.[2]

The award was modified for the 2023 season: fans can select from a shortlist of the best on-track action every month and vote for their favourite overtake. At the end of the season, all winners are voted to decide the definitive on-track move of the season.[3]

Chronology of Overtake Awards[edit]

Overtake Award 2021[edit]

In the inaugural Crypto.com Overtake Award, Sebastian Vettel has won the award for making the most overtakes of any driver during the 2021 season.[4][5]

Overtake King 2022[edit]

In 2022, the award was awarded after every race. At the end of the season, Sebastian Vettel's overtake against Kevin Magnussen was voted as the favorite on-track move of the season, winning the award for two years in a row.[6]

Overtake of the Month 2023[edit]

In 2023, the fans select from a shortlist of the best on-track action every month of the season. At the end of the season, all monthly winners went up against each other, and Charles Leclerc's overtake against Sergio Perez was awarded as the Overtake of the Year.[3]
