Racing car constructor
For the surname, see Pankratz (surname).
This article does not cite any sources. Please help improve this article by adding citations to reliable sources. Unsourced material may be challenged and removed.
Find sources: "Pankratz" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (June 2008) (Learn how and when to remove this template message)

Pankratz was a racing car constructor. Pankratz cars competed in two FIA World Championship races - the 1954 and 1955 Indy 500.
