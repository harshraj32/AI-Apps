MTV Force India The Fast and The Gorgeous is a reality show by MTV India produced in association with Indian Formula 1 team Force India.[1][2] The show’s concept was to find four new face of Force India F1 for the 2009 season.[3][4][5]

The show was hosted by the VJ Rannvijay Singh.

MTV India did not commission a second series. In 2011, Force India partnered with NDTV Good Times for a second season, presented by Ambika Anand.[6]
